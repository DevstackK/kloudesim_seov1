## Coverage and networks in Japan

Your KloudeSIM Japan eSIM connects to NTT Docomo and SoftBank, two of Japan's three major national carriers. Both operate extensive 4G LTE networks across Honshu, Kyushu, Shikoku, and Hokkaido, with strong signal in Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto, Hiroshima, and other major tourist and business destinations. The eSIM automatically selects whichever of the two networks has the stronger signal at your location, so you're not tied to a single carrier's gaps. In practice, having access to both networks on one eSIM provides more reliable connectivity than a single-carrier option — particularly useful in a country as geographically varied as Japan.

## How to install your Japan eSIM

1. Buy your plan at kloudesim.com — your QR code arrives by email within minutes of purchase.
2. On iPhone: Settings → Mobile Data → Add eSIM → Use QR Code. On Android: Settings → Connections → SIM Manager → Add Mobile Plan, then scan the code.
3. Label the new line something like "Japan KloudeSIM" so it's easy to identify when you're managing two active SIMs.
4. Set the KloudeSIM line as your preferred data line and confirm that data roaming is turned on for it. Your original SIM stays active for calls and texts throughout.
5. Board your flight. When you land in Japan, your phone connects to NTT Docomo or SoftBank automatically — nothing else to do.
6. Running low on data? Top up through the KloudeSIM app without interrupting your connection.

Your device needs to support eSIM and be unlocked from your home carrier. Check the [KloudeSIM compatibility checker](/compatibility) before you buy if you're not certain about your handset.

## Frequently asked questions

**Will my eSIM work as soon as I land in Japan?**

Yes. Because you install the eSIM before you travel, your phone connects to a Japanese network the moment you switch it on after landing. There's nothing to activate at the airport and no queueing at a kiosk — your data line is already there, ready to go.

**Can I hotspot/tether with a KloudeSIM Japan eSIM?**

Yes — all KloudeSIM Japan plans include full, unrestricted tethering. Connect your laptop, tablet, or a travel companion's phone to your mobile hotspot at no extra cost. This is one area where KloudeSIM and Airalo both clearly beat Holafly, which caps hotspot use at just 500MB per day regardless of how much data you have remaining.

**Can I keep my WhatsApp number?**

Yes. KloudeSIM is a data-only eSIM. Your original SIM stays active in the same phone, handling calls and SMS as normal, so WhatsApp, iMessage, and regular phone calls all work exactly as they do at home. You don't need to share a temporary Japanese number with anyone.

**What happens when my data runs out?**

You can top up from the KloudeSIM app while you're still in Japan. And your 30-day validity period starts when you first activate the eSIM in Japan, not when you bought it — so purchasing a few days before your trip doesn't cost you any time.
